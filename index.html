How does it work?

AI Alpha gives traders a competitive edge to make more profitable trades. 



This is conducting the technical, market and fundamental analysis across different time periods to analyse when is the right time to enter a trade based on predefined trading strategies.

Once a potential position is identified, our MLP will evaluate the market conditions and the history of entering such a position before generating a signal.

Why AI Alpha?

Our mission is to simplify the ability of gaining information on how to trade crypto to grow the adoption of it, as well as showing individuals how to make money from it.



Our Alpha Involves More Than Just Generating Profitable Signals

We are obsessed with risk management, so for every trade signal we provide we will always advice on: (a) the accuracy rating of the signal (b) multiple take profits (c) a range for setting your stop loss (d) a range of entry points (e) advice on how much leverage you should use

Ensure that we provide regular market updates that look beyond using just Technical Analysis. Our algorithm already takes into account macroeconomic events, fundamental analysis and more, and our AI bot will pass on key information that will help assist traders far beyond just sending signals into the Telegram group
