Our Story



The Problem(s) With The Current Crypto Trading Environment

We started as a team of cryptocurrency traders frustrated with the current crypto trading ecosystem of sub-standard signals with little to no guidance in relation to risk management, a lack of transparency in relation to the driving forces behind price movements of cryptocurrencies and the overly complex trading interfaces of CEXs and DEXs.

Our Initial Solution

1. Educational and actionable content.
2. Simplifying the key building blocks of crypto trading. 
3. Developing consistently profitable signals, weekly trading guides and more in order to give our community all the information they need in one place to become profitable traders.

The coming of age of AI & MLP

While AI exploded into mainstream attention at the start of 2023, our trading team expanded to include AI & MLP developers and programmers last year to start building bespoke trading strategies that took our years of crypto trading data in order to construct consistently profitable trading signals.  And over the past three months we have gone one step further to create an AI virtual assistant that will give guidance to our community so that we can go above and beyond the current level of information provided by paid crypto signal groups. Our AI Assistant will educate, inform and generate unique signals. The Alpha provided will make our community not only make more profitable trades but also gain the Alpha for making their own trades as well.
